Authorize Sage, eBay, and Amazon once. Tokens will be stored in BigQuery and used automatically by your Python pipelines.

Use these as redirect URLs in provider portals (replace https://your-domain.com with your app's URL):
https://your-domain.com/oauth/callback/sage, https://your-domain.com/oauth/callback/ebay, https://your-domain.com/oauth/callback/amazon
